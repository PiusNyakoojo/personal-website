Hi, welcome to my personal website!

My name is Pius and I love learning new things and applying that knowledge to make cool stuff.

In my free time I enjoy making games for the web, experimenting, jogging, playing tennis or eating green tea ice cream.

You can find most of the interesting software-related projects I’ve worked on in this Portfolio.

My Lab contains documentation of interesting experiments that I run. I expect most of the experiments will be related to genetics, acoustic physics and robotics; but I can’t promise that something like plant morphology or entomology won’t be there, as those are quite interesting subjects as well.

To bring everything together is what this Blog is for. Here I’ll talk about whatever’s on my mind as if no one is listening.

I feel that it’s very important to empower ourselves through sharing alternate perspectives. When we empower ourselves, we unconsciously give others the permission to do the same.

Quote of the day
